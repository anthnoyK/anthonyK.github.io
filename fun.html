Maybe you and your team want to have some bonding outside of official events? If so, there are a variety of experiences in Orlando for you and your team.

Everyone loves a good puzzle, and Escapeology's Escape rooms are certainly a good puzzle. Enjoy some thrilling, team building action!

Escape Rooms

If you're stressed out, you can have some retail therapy at Mall at Millenia. This is the premier mall for designer clothing in Orlando.

Designer Mall

Perhaps you'd like to indulge your more refined side with some fine art. Orlando Museum of Art has a fine collection of paintings, sculputures and architecture.

Orlando Museum of Art

If you're more of a solo person, there are still plenty of attractions for the adventurous souls. Try out these thrills while in Orlando!

Humans have always wanted to fly, and without a pilot's liscense, this may be the closest you can get. If you have a strong stomach, try out a hot air ballon ride at the website below.

Hot Air Ballon Rides

If you felt like jumping out of the hot air balloon and doing some skydiving, here's a certifiably safer way to feel the wind against your body. Check out IFlyWorld for Indoor Skydiving!

Indoor Skydiving

Quad rides are sure to bring some adrenaline in your veins, and nature is good for the soul. Give yourself a thrill ride at Revolution Offroad!

Offroad Quad

After the day's agenda, you might be tired and just need a place away from your hotel to relax. Here are a couple relaxing things to do.

It's been scientifically proven that walking reduces stress and makes you happier, so why not take a calm walk around Lake Eola? Have some fresh air and detox from the day's events.

Lake Eola Park

Thinking of a place to eat, drink, and read? Try Sleeping Moon Cafe. The atmosphere is perfect for some reading.

Sleeping Moon Cafe

But if you want to be relaxed to the max, a massage from the Salt Room will definitely do it. The spa is renowned for their experiences, so be sure to check it out.

The Salt Room
